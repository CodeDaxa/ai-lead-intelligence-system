## Overview

AI Lead Intelligence System is a business automation platform that extracts, processes, and analyzes company data to create actionable business intelligence.

It helps businesses reduce manual research work by automating lead discovery, data enrichment, and reporting workflows.


## Business Problem

Companies spend hours manually:

- Searching business information
- Collecting contact data
- Cleaning spreadsheets
- Researching potential customers
- Preparing outreach lists

This system automates that workflow.


## Solution

The automation pipeline:

1. Extract business data from public sources

2. Process and clean collected information

3. Analyze companies using AI

4. Generate structured intelligence reports

5. Export ready-to-use business datasets
